University of Leicester Redesign

Roles: Information Architect/UX Researcher, Front-End Dev
Duration: October 2014 - November 2014

Description
The university page redesign was done as a study of usability and information architecture for COGS 187a. In a weeks time, we rebuilt the poorly designed site, specifically addressing the landing page and pages on the paths to admissions and applying for financial aid.
↳ The original, confusing layout of their site.

↳ Our more conventional, less confusing revamp.

Approach
The problem we had to solve was to identify what were the challenges of using the original university site, and resolve these issues in our redesign. To do this, each team member went on the site and individually went through the pages to determine usability issues. We created personas of potential users of the site and wrote specific use cases that they may be there for. Then, we went through the pages as each persona type and evaluated the difficulty and number of clicks it required to reach each destination.

Next, we went on the websites of five other university pages to examine their layout, what links were made available on each page, and how they chose to present their information. We created a competitive analysis to assess what is considered the convention for home page links, the type of navigation, and the structure of the site. With this data, we came up with a cross between the competitors' sites and our own ideas as the general idea of how we wanted our redesign to be.

Process
With our collected information, we sketched up our redesign, the created wireframes for the pages that were to be coded. We wrote the code to set up the home page as well as second and third pages that were clicked through on the way to finding out more about financial aid. For other main pages that were not created with code, we created high fidelity wireframes to demonstrate what the page would look like.

Finally, we wrote about the hardest decisions made during the design process. With everything completed, we finalized the site and prepared to present our redesign. Our presentation addressed the improvements in our redesign, the heuristics we addressed, and more explanation about specific design choices.

Deliverables
1 Usability Evaluation Site
2 Competitive Analysis
3 Use Cases
4 Hardest Decisions
5 Live Redesign
