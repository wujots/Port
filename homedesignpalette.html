Home Palette Design

Description
This is a personal project I'd like to present as an overview of the process I take when designing a project from start to finish. HDP is web application that facilitates online purchases of home furnishings by helping users color match potential buys with the palettes of their rooms.

The Challenge
During my time at BednLinens, an online shop for home furnishings such as comforters and curtains, part of my responsibiities included customer service. I spent a large portion of my time responding to messages from buyers about their purchases. One of the common issues I noticed was that the customer would request a return on their purchase because upon the product's arrival, the customer would realize that the color of the product did not fit the color scheme of the room they wished to use it in. I realized these complaints could easily be alleviated if there were a simple way for the customer to match the color of the product to the color of their room.
